## Summary

Review of three voice mode bug fixes (black screen on back button, STT silent after ElevenLabs TTS, orchestrator post-dispose guards) and a new 472-line voice mode integration test. One blocking finding identified and resolved in-review: `_updateState()` lacked a `_disposed` guard, leaving ~50 call sites vulnerable to post-dispose crashes. Seven advisory findings for follow-up.
