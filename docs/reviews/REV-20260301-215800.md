## Summary

Reviewed the Knowledge Amplification Pipeline (Phases 4-6): 10 new Python scripts, 4 new SQLite tables, 2 views, schema migrations, 5 modified commands, 1 new command, and CLAUDE.md updates. The pipeline design is architecturally sound — layer boundaries are maintained, failure isolation is correct, and idempotency is ensured via UNIQUE constraints and INSERT OR IGNORE throughout. Two blocking findings must be addressed: a missing ADR and a wiring gap where `mine_patterns.py` is not called from `close_discussion.py`.
